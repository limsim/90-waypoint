Waypoint Creator

60px

Auto-generated: The app starts with 20 waypoints automatically generated following all rules.

First waypoint: Click anywhere on the canvas to place it.

Subsequent waypoints: Click near the last waypoint to place the next one in the closest cardinal direction (North, South, East, or West) at a random distance.

Variable distances: Each waypoint is placed 60-140 pixels away from the previous one, creating varied spacing.

Configurable minimum distance: Use the slider above to adjust the minimum distance between waypoints (20-100px).

No Overlaps: Waypoints will never overlap with existing waypoints. If your preferred direction would overlap, the system will try alternative cardinal directions.

Use "Add Random Waypoint" to add individual waypoints, "Regenerate 20 Waypoints" to create a new set, or "Clear All" to start over.
